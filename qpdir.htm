Direction to Crewe Queens Park and Platform 1 Skate park Tipkinder Crewe from the M6.

If you get lost ask for the Queens park as everyone knows that.

Click on the map for a full screen map.

From the Motorway leave the M6 at junction 16 Crewe if you are coming from the south turn left onto the A500, if you are coming from the North go around the roundabout and take the 1st left after the motorway turning (in effect you are turning right as you come off the motorway but you have to go around the roundabout). Its Sign posted for Crewe

 If you are coming from SOT junction 16 is the end of the A500.

Keep straight on for about a mile  until you reach the next roundabout keep in the right hand lane and then turn right onto Old Park Rd.

At the next roundabout turn left onto Weston   Rd.

Across another roundabout and you will start seeing factories and offices on the right one of them is Caudwell on your left you will see Gallaher's cigarette factory. Through the first set of lights and keep in the left hand lane. You will see the new Tesco warehouse.

 

You then have 3 sets of lights with the post office on your left and B&Q on your right keep in the left lane. At the last set of lights you will see the roundabout with the Crewe Arms Hotel in front of you turn left onto Nantwich rd.

You will see the station on your left now follow the instruction on how to get from the railway station.

  

Directions from Crewe Railway Station to the Queens Park and Platform 1 Skatepark

Click on the map for a full screen map.

This map follows on from the directions from the M6.

Click on any picture for a full screen picture

 

As you come out of the railway station you will see the Crewe Arms Hotel on your right and a roundabout. Turn left onto Nantwich Rd through the first set of lights which are Pelican. You will see Railhouse in front of you (the big tall building)
At the next set of lights keep in the right hand lane and at the cross roads turn right down Mill St You will see Adecco office in red on your right
Speed Camera are down this road watch out its a 30mph speed limit. You start to go down a hill and then there is a railway bridge which you go under and there is a roundabout straight in front of you turn left past Farm Foods. This is Oak St
At the lights go straight across onto Wisaston Rd and straight across the next lights which are immediately after the first set.
Keep going straight through the next set of lights at the junction of Flag Lane.  There is a shop on one corner and a pub on the other
You will see a few garages on your right then a turning on the left for Stewart St go passed this turning
Keep going straight. On your left just after Stewart st you will see a builders yard and a scrap metal yard Houstons. A few feet after that on your left you will see a break in the trees and a foot path with the BMX track straight in front of you. Go in there and the park is on the right.
If you are in a car you can park on the road or keep going 100 yards and turn left immediately after the garage on your left
A few yards on your left as you go around the park you will see the Queens Park Car park you can park there.
